Demo 8 — Universal blocking via interceptRuntime

Exercises the productionised runtime patches (ADR-0013 Phase 2). One real SimpleCMP.init({ interceptRuntime: true }) call wires the patches into the engine: when consent for a configured service isn't granted, JS-injected calls to its origins are blocked. Toggle consent via the banner / floating trigger and re-run the buttons — blocked calls flip to allowed without re-init.

Configured services in this demo: analytics (origins: tracker.example.test, *.tracker.example.test) and videos (origins: *.video.example.test). Hosts outside those patterns fall through as unknown (= pass through).
